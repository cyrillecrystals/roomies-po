ROOMIES

This is a website where people moving to new places can get affordable housing. The houses are shared, so the tenants share pay the rent. The user can also upload their property for listing. Roomies Website is based on targetting people with space to share with others.

Going to school? Hostels aren't your thing? Would you prefer a community of like-minded students to not just live with but share experiences with? Then you're in the right place. Look through our listings put up by students just like you, looking to cost share affordable,secure housing just click to create an account and start on your search. We care for your financial levels.
